About Catpanion

They stay while you work or play!

Catpanion is a game with a transparent feature, So you can play any game or use any application on your pc without interuption! We wanted to allow your cats to be a part of your digital day without being too intrusive on your daily activities.

Lots of cats!

Catpanion allows you to adopt multiple cat companions to keep you company! With many cats having their own personalities and differing colours, they will always keep you entertained while you go about your day!

Frequently Asked Questions

Your quick stop for quick support! Check out this information here!

What platforms is Catpanion available on?

Catpanion is currently only available on Windows based platforms that support DirectX 11.

At this current time the Catpanion game does not work natively on Mac OS or Linux 

How to get future updates?

At this current time we have two ways of updating Catpanion.

Catpanion enhanced will have automatic updating through steam, you have nothing to worry about with updating on this version!

Catpanion basic will require the user to manually download the updated files, and drag over their save data to the new version's game folder. Catpanion will handle the rest from there!

How do I decide where my catpanions can go?

Catpanions can currently be set to display on a singular display (if you have multiple monitors). In order to define a specific area for your catpanions to reside, you must select the display you wish to be on in the settings menu, and then you can control the play area using the border define settings.

These settings will allow you to set a specific box of play area using 4 lines acting as walls, the floor, and a roof.
